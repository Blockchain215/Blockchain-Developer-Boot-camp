Welcome!

Hello and welcome to ConsenSys Academy’s Blockchain Developer Bootcamp, where you will learn everything you need to know to become a world-class blockchain developer.

This course will give you the tools to be a blockchain developer today. However, the blockchain field develops at an incredibly fast pace. Anyone working in the space is in a continuous onboarding process.

With that in mind, this course aims to teach from a first principles approach. We hope this gives you a deeper understanding of the material that can accommodate the rapid change occurring in the blockchain world.

Course Content

Below is the general outline of the course:

  • ⬩ Introduction and Setup
  • ⬩ Blockchain Fundamentals
  • ⬩ Ethereum Basics
  • ⬩ Solidity from Scratch
  • ⬩ Writing Smart Contracts
  • ⬩ Smart Contract Pitfalls, Testing and Debugging
  • ⬩ Development Frameworks and Environment
  • ⬩ Decentralized Finance
  • ⬩ Additional Topics
  • ⬩ Advanced Topics
